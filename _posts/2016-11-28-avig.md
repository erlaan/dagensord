Avig = Inside Out

----

### What does it mean?

**Avig** is the Swedish word for inside out.

----

### Examples

\- Din tröja ser konstig ut!
\- Ja, den är **avig**.

_\- Your shirt looks weird!_  
_\- Yes, it's "inside out"._
