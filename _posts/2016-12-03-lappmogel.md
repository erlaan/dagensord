Lappmögel = Snow

----

### What does it mean?

**Lappmögel** is a Swedish word for snow. Used in the counties of Halland and Skåne.

----

### Examples

\- Nu kommer lappmöglet igen!  
\- Mm, jävla lappmögel.  

_\- Here comes the snow again!_  
_\- Mm, fucking snow"._
